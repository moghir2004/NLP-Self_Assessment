Via restricting the set:
$$
\begin{align}
    P(REL|INV) &= \frac{P(REL \cap INV)}{P(INV)}\\
    &= \frac{\frac{18}{1179}}{\frac{69}{1179}} \\
    &= \frac{18}{69}
\end{align}
$$
$$
\begin{align}
    P(INV|REL) &= \frac{P(INV \cap REL)}{P(REL)}\\
    &= \frac{\frac{18}{1179}}{\frac{58}{1179}} \\
    &= \frac{18}{58}
\end{align}
$$

Via Bayes' Theorem
$$
\begin{align}
    P(INV|REL) &= \frac{P(REL|INV)\cdot P(INV)}{P(REL)} \\
    &= \frac{\frac{18}{69}\cdot \frac{69}{1179}}{\frac{58}{1179}}\\
    &= \frac{18}{58}

\end{align}
$$
